About Goldnex

Marketing agencies sell activity. We sell outcomes.

Goldnex Media started as a two-person media buying shop frustrated with agencies that reported on impressions instead of income. Today we run acquisition for over a hundred brands — with the same obsession over what the spend actually returns.

Our story

Built by media buyers, not consultants.

Every milestone below marks a shift in how we work, not just how big we got.

2018

Founded in a rented desk

Goldnex began managing paid search for three local retailers, reinvesting every dollar of profit into learning what actually drove sales.

2020

First seven-figure client

A DTC skincare brand scaled from $40K to $1.2M in monthly ad spend with us, without losing efficiency — our first proof the model held at scale.

2022

Added SEO & content as a core discipline

Clients kept asking why we didn't own organic too. We built an in-house editorial and technical SEO team instead of outsourcing it.

2025

120+ brands, one standard

We now manage eight figures in annual media spend across finance, retail, and health — still reviewed weekly against revenue, not reach.
